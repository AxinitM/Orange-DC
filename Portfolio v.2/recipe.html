Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hour
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 40 minutes

Ingredients for 8 portions

  • 400gr of store-bought or homemade dough
  • 8 thinly sliced apples
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 200gr sugar (a blend of white and brown sugar)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Optional fillings: nuts, raisin and other dried fruits, vanilla, honey etc.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 230°C.
  2. Divide the dough into two roughly equal portions. Place the first portion in a pie dish; set the second one aside.
  3. Combine all sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Add more sugar if your apples are sour or tart.
  4. Layer apple slices in the prepared pie dish, sprinkling each layer with cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  5. Roll out the second part of the dough and cut 5cm wide strips (lines).
  6. Dot top layer of the apple slices small pieces of butter.
  7. Lay out strips of dough in two levels. Place the second level of strips at 90 degrees to the first level.
  8. Bake the pie on the lowest rack of the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
  9. Reduce oven temperature to 175°C and continue baking until golden brown colour (30 to 35 minutes more).
  10. Serve warm or cold, with Ice-Cream or Whipping Cream if needed.
